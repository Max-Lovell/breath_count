This task is about counting your breathing. In this task, you need to repeatedly count your breaths from 1 to 9. An in and out breath together count as one single breath.


So we can keep track of your breathing, the task here is to press the UP arrow on your keyboard for breaths 1-8 in a sequence, once for each breath. On the 9th breath, press the DOWN arrow on your keyboard.


Then, indicate your confidence that you kept count of your breath correctly - after the 9th breath, press the RIGHT arrow if you are certain you counted correctly, or press the LEFT arrow if you are not sure you pressed the correct arrow.


If you are sure you have lost count of your breathing, please press the SPACEBAR to start counting from 1 again.


There is a one minute practice session with feedback on your responses (the order you pressed the keys in), and the main experimental task will last 10 minutes and will not give you feedback.


You don't have to close your eyes for this task, but please only count in your head and not on your fingers. You don't have to control your breathing and can just breathe naturally.
